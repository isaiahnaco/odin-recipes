Lumpiang Shanghai Recipe

Description

Pork Sisig is a fantastic dish if you're looking for a rich, meaty dish. Great for parties or a relaxing dinner, this meal is a little more complex than the other recipes. However, it is very worth the effort.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Minced Onions
  • Bay Leaves
  • Ginger
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Black Pepper
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar
  • Liver Spread
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Boil your pork, ginger, and bay leaves in a cooking pot filled with water and salt. After one hour, drain the water.
  2. Grill your pork for a few minutes per side. Afterwards, let it cool down.
  3. While the meat is cooling down, prepare the dressing by mixing mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt, liver spread, limes, and black pepper.
  4. Chop the pork into small pieces. Combine the chopped pork with the onions and the dressing. Mix them all together until they are well blended.
  5. Transfer your sisig to a sizzling hot plate. Crack open an egg and let it cook over the sisig. Serve and enjoy!
