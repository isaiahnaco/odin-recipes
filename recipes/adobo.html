Pork Adobo Recipe

Description

This recipe is for the world famous Filipino Adobo, specifically the one made with pork. This dish is a personal favorite of mine. Whenever my mom prepares it, my day is suddenly much brighter.

What makes Adobo so interesting is that the steps to make it can be played with while still resulting in a delicious meal. However, this recipe will follow a very general procedure, something that you can have fun with on your own after.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Pepper Corn
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Dried Bay Leaves
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Combine the pork, soy sauce, sugar, and garlic in a large bowl. Marinade for at least an hour, but preferrably overnight
  2. Heat up a pan or pot and toss in the marinated pork. Sear the pork for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in the rest of the marinade.
  4. Add water, pepper corn, and dried bay leaves. Bring to a boil then simmer for at least 40 minutes.
  5. Pour in vinegar and simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
