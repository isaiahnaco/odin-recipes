Lumpiang Shanghai Recipe

Description

Another famous Filipino dish, Lumpiang Shanghai is often found in any kind of pinoy party. It's always a race to get a share of the lumpia, especially when I'm there.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Minced Onions
  • Minced Carrots
  • Lumpia Wrapper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Eggs
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Combine the ground pork, minced onions, minced carrots, garlic powder, black pepper, salt, sesame oil, and eggs into a bowl and mix well. This will serve as your filling.
  2. Scoop some of the filling and place it over a sheet of lumpia wrapper. Spread it and fold the wrapper until you attain its iconic shape.
  3. Fill a pan or pot with oil and heat it. Deep fry your lumpia until they are a golden brown.
  4. Put your lumpia in a strainer of some kind and let the excess oil drip. Serve and enjoy!
